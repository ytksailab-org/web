Join us

Saito Lab is a cross-institute laboratory between Kitasato University, AIST & The University of Tokyo. We are looking for graduate students and postdoc researchers to join us! Also feel free to cantact with Saito: saito.yutaka AT kitasato-u.ac.jp (replace AT with @).

Join as a graduate student

Join our laboratory as a graduate student at Department of Biodata Science, Graduate School of Frontier Engineering, Kitasato University, or Department of Computational Biology and Medical Sciences, Graduate School of Frontier Science, The University of Tokyo. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds. Students can select different research styles including (1) develop new machine learning and/or bioinformatics methods, (2) analyze publicly available data for new biological findings, and (3) analyze data in a collaborative work with experimental biologists.

Admission information:

  • Department of Biodata Science, Graduate School of Frontier Engineering, Kitasato University.
  • Department of Computational Biology and Medical Sciences, Graduate School of Frontier Science, The University of Tokyo.
